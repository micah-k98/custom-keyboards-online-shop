About Us

Welcome to Custom Keys Enthusiasts where we transform ordinary keyboards into extraordinary tools for your personal and professional needs. We're passionate about crafting custom keyboards that are not only functional but also a reflection of your unique style and preferences.

Our Story

At Custom Keys Enthusiasts , our journey began with a simple realization: off-the-shelf keyboards don't always cater to the diverse needs and desires of individuals. We recognized the increasing demand for personalized and high-quality mechanical keyboards, and our mission was born. Our founders, Micah Kashiwabara, shared a common vision of creating a space where keyboard enthusiasts, artists, and tech lovers could collaborate to bring your dream keyboard to life.

Your Vision, Our Creation

At Custom Keys Enthusiasts , we believe in the power of personalization. Whether you're a gamer, a programmer, a designer, or anyone who values a quality keyboard, we're here to make your vision a reality. Our custom keyboards can be tailored to your specific needs--from the key switches to the keycap designs and even the lighting effects. Your keyboard, your rules.
